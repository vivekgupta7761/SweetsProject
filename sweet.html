    Welcome to Rohtash's Sweets

    At Rohtash's Sweets, we're passionate about bringing you the finest traditional sweets crafted with love and care. Founded by Rohtash Singh, a master confectioner with over 30 years of experience, our sweet shop has become a beloved destination for those seeking the authentic taste of India.

    Our sweets are made fresh daily, ensuring that each bite is a delightful experience. We take pride in using traditional methods and natural ingredients, with no artificial preservatives or additives.
    Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply indulging your sweet tooth, Rohtash's Sweets offers a taste of authenticity that is sure to delight.
